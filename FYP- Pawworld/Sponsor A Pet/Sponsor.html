Sponsor A Pet
At Paw World AWS Animal Welfare Society , you have the opportunity to make a direct and meaningful impact on the life of a pet in need through our "Sponsor a Pet" program. Some of those pets at our shelter require more time and attention than others. This can be for a variety of reasons, such as behavioural or medical issues.

By sponsoring a pet, not only you will change their life, but you'll also change the lives of many others in desperate situations. Your support will ensure that these pets receive the care they need and deserve.

When you join the Sponsor Club you will receive regular updates from your sponsor pet throughout the year. Your gift of sponsorship will help fund the shelter, food, medicine and veterinary care for dogs that have nowhere else to go and is used not only to help your sponsored dog but all the other animals Paw World cares for.
