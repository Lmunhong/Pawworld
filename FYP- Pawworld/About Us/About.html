About Us
Our Story
Paw World Animal Welfare Society is a non-profit animal shelter in Petaling Jaya that has been in operation since 2022. We receive the surrender of unwanted dogs and cats which we will vaccinate, deworm, neuter/spay, and put up for adoption.
Currently, there are over 30 dogs and 15 cats under the care of the shelter. The shelter and all costs involved in running it are entirely funded by the generous donations of the public as well as proceeds from charitable events. Our team consists of four office staff, a number
of part-time veterinarians, one vet assistant, seven kennel workers,
and one driver.
We believe in transparency, responsibility, and the power of education. Through advocacy and outreach programs, we promote responsible pet ownership and highlight the joy and benefits of adopting a rescue pet. Vision
Together, we strive to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets of all breeds and ages. Each adoption is a celebration of new beginnings, where pets find loving families and families find their perfect companions.
Mission
To connect loving homes with deserving pets, ensuring every animal gets a chance to thrive in a safe and caring environment.
